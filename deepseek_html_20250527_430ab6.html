About Dumming

Founded in 1995, Dumming has revolutionized the adhesive industry with our patented bonding technology. What began as a small chemical research lab has grown into the world's leading glue manufacturer.

Our secret lies in the proprietary polymer formula developed by our founder, Dr. Emma Dumming. Unlike ordinary adhesives, Dumming glue forms molecular-level bonds that become stronger over time.

Today, our products are used by professionals and homeowners in over 85 countries. From spacecraft assembly to children's art projects, Dumming provides the perfect bonding solution for every application.

All Dumming products are non-toxic, environmentally friendly, and manufactured in our ISO-certified facilities.
